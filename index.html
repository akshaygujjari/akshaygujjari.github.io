About Me

Hello, I am Akshay Gujjari pursuing a master's in Computer Science specializing in Data Analytics at the State University of New York, Albany. I am currently, working as a Data Analyst for the New York State Department of Environmental Conservation for Forest Health Team. I am seeking full-time opportunities from May, 2020 in the field of Data Science. I am passionate about Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Software Development, Software Engineering, and Product Development. Previously, I have worked as a Data Research Analyst for the Center for Technology in Government on a project named Prototype Development for Water Quality Analysis in New York State. I have 2.5 years of experience working as an Associate Developer for Vistex on the SAP ABAP Module. My work at Vistex allowed me to explore various things alongside programming and that's where I found that it's the analysis of data that keeps me motivated.

I believe that “Analytics For All” and that is why I have always strived to explore various concepts of Analytics. I believe that there have to be some findings from the data to be analyzed and we should be able to understand the story that the data is trying to tell us. In a nutshell, I have a huge experience of handling many types of Data, such as, structured and unstructured. I possess a strong knowledge of Python, R, SQL, Tableau, ArcGIS, Data Mining, Machine Learning, Data Analytics, Data Visualization, Data Management.

I am volunteering as a Sports Chair for Indian Student Organization at SUNY Albany. Indian Student Organization is one of the largest Student Organization. My hobbies include playing Volleyball, Cricket, and Swimming.
